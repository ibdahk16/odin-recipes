Banana Pudding

The best banana pudding recipe stolen off the internet

This recipe was created by my grandma Judy who gave me this recipe as part of my inheritance, we used to always have this at granny's house until, well, we couldn't. My brother got the 20 thousand dollar inheritance but I'll happily take the pudding.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups of cold milks
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 container of frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 package of vanilla wafers
  • 14 bananas, sliced

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat pudding mix and milk for two minutes
  2. Blend in condensed milk until smooth
  3. Stir in vanilla and fold in whipping topping
  4. Layer wafers, bananas, and pudding mixture in a glass serving bowl
  5. Chill until serving
